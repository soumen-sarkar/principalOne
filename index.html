About Principle One

Principle One is multi-disciplinary, multi-cultural, multi-lingual company company of technologists that employs over 100 people and has offices across the region including in Hong Kong, Tokyo, Singapore and a presence in Shanghai. We make it easier for businesses to set up and run their advanced Audiovisual and Information Technology to the highest standards. Our multi-lingual, multi-cultural approach makes us the partner of choice for many international companies.

We have a wide range of clients, both from Asia and from elsewhere in the world.  They all demand a consistently high standard of project and service delivery, often in multiple locations in Asia. We work with organisations in many different industries, notably the alternative investment and luxury retail sectors.

Services

Principle One is an IT and audiovisual services company that designs, integrates and operates complete solutions for technology infrastructure, encompassing computers, phones, video conferencing, digital signage and the networks that connect them. Principle One provides desk-side, in-store and remote support for these solutions and to the users who rely on them throughout the region.

Principle One’s approach makes the organisation the partner of choice for a wide range of clients, both from Asia and from elsewhere in the world from many different industries, notably the alternative investment and luxury retail sectors.

Principle One’s is structured in 5 distinct business units:

IT Managed Services

IT Managed Services operates and supports clients’ IT infrastructure and the people who rely on it. This includes both desk-side and remote support with rapid response times, plus regular reviews and strategic advice.

IT Projects

IT Projects works with clients to design and implement their IT infrastructure so that it fulfils all current requirements and can grow to meet changing business needs.

AV Maintenance

AV Managed Services operates and supports clients’ audiovisual solutions for meeting rooms, training facilities and auditoria on an ongoing basis.  This service also includes also planning and running special events, both in clients’ premises and at external venues.

AV Projects

AV Projects designs, builds and installs audiovisual systems for clients. These systems are functional, user friendly and creative, and are installed with meticulous attention to detail.

AV Managed Services

AV Maintenance  provides preventative care, repairs and component replacement, plus the extension of manufacturer’s warranties, to ensure that clients’ audiovisual systems are consistently reliable and fit for purpose.
